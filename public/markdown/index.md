## Why work with us

Most agencies optimize for their own convenience and bill you for it. Here is how we have decided to work instead, in writing.

1. **Best tech for your needs, not ours.** We pick the stack that fits your project and the people who will maintain it, not the one we happen to be fastest at. You never pay for our habits.
2. **Weekly live demos and updates.** Every week you get a working build to click through and a plain-English rundown of what changed. No month-long silences. No surprise invoices.
3. **Live platform from day one.** Your platform ships to production with the first build. You watch it grow feature by feature instead of waiting for one big reveal at the end.
4. **Build only what you will use.** We cut anything that does not earn its place. No bloated feature lists you will never open, just the things your users actually reach for.
5. **Your feedback shapes everything.** Your input drives every decision. We do not disappear for months. You are part of the process from day one.

## How we work

1. **First call.** Tell us about your vision: what you are building, who it is for, and what is holding you back right now. We listen first.
2. **Plan the next feature.** We decide what to build next, what matters most, and what will make the biggest difference for your learners.
3. **Design it.** We design how it looks and how it works. You see exactly what it will be before we write a line of code.
4. **Build it.** We bring it to life, handle the heavy lifting, and make sure it works as it should.
5. **Demo it.** You get a live version to test, break, and react to.
6. **Launch it.** We ship it to your platform. Your users get it.

## Questions answered

### How quickly can you build our platform?

It depends on scope. We break work into weekly sprints with live demos, so you see progress every week rather than vague updates. Most platforms launch in phases: core features first, usually in 8–12 weeks, then we iterate based on what real users show us. You get a clear roadmap after our first conversation.

### How involved will we be during development?

Very. Every week you see what has been built, test it live, and give feedback that shapes the next sprint. You are part of the process from day one.

### Can you integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. Payment gateways, CRMs, email platforms, Zoom, Google Meet, SSO, and the tools you currently use can be connected. We build around your workflow, not against it.

### What happens after we launch?

Launch is the beginning. Optional maintenance contracts cover support, updates, new features, and optimization as the platform evolves.

### Will the platform scale as we grow?

The platform is architected for growth from day one, from 500 learners to 50,000, with infrastructure adjusted as you scale.

### Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Yes. It works on desktop, tablet, and mobile, responsively by default.

### Who owns the code and platform?

You do. Full ownership of the code, platform, and infrastructure transfers on final payment. Maintenance is optional.
