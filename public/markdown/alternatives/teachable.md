# Teachable alternative for a custom course platform

**Comparison page:** [/alternatives/teachable](/alternatives/teachable)
**Related service:** [/services/course-platform-for-creators](/services/course-platform-for-creators)

Teachable is faster and cheaper for a straightforward course catalogue. A custom platform earns its cost when checkout, bundles, memberships, team purchases, or learner journeys have become recurring operational exceptions.

## Why creators leave

- Course bundles, memberships, applications, affiliate rules, and regional tax flows become exceptions.
- The brand can stop where product settings begin.
- Commerce and reporting need to follow the business rather than a plan.

## Where Teachable wins

Use it to launch a conventional course quickly or when you do not want to own software operations.

## Comparison

| Dimension | Rizon | Teachable |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Commerce | Checkout and payments designed around your offer and provider | Built-in commerce and plan-dependent options |
| Code | Application code and deployment handover | Vendor-operated platform |
| Cost | Build cost plus chosen services | Subscription and payment fees under the current plan |

## Migration and FAQ

Export available course assets and customer records, then test purchase, enrolment, access, refund, and support handoff before cutover. A custom platform can use the payment provider you choose; the money lands in your account without a platform cut above processor fees. A focused release normally takes **8–12 weeks**.
