# Docebo alternative for custom enterprise training

**Comparison page:** [/alternatives/docebo](/alternatives/docebo)
**Related service:** [/services/corporate-training-platform](/services/corporate-training-platform)

Docebo is credible for organisations that need an enterprise LMS without building one. Custom work is for training that is part of the product: customer education, partner enablement, or proprietary certification joined to account health, entitlement, or renewal data.

## Why teams leave

- Training needs product-like workflows for customers or partners.
- Learning signals must join core systems, not arrive as a periodic export.
- The business case requires control of the roadmap.

## Where Docebo wins

Keep Docebo when requirements fit an enterprise LMS and vendor-managed delivery is the better trade. A custom build costs more upfront and needs active product ownership.

## Comparison

| Dimension | Rizon | Docebo |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Enterprise workflow | Built around customers, partners, and operations | Configured enterprise LMS capabilities |
| Roadmap | Priorities follow your business case | Shared vendor roadmap plus configuration |
| Operations | Code and deployment decisions handed over | Vendor-operated LMS and agreement-based services |

## Migration and FAQ

Treat migration as a system integration: identity, content, enrolment rules, completion records, integrations, and reporting each receive an owner and acceptance test. The aim is a controlled cutover, not a heroic weekend.
