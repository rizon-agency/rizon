# Custom Course Platform for Creators

**For:** established creators whose checkout, member experience, and learner flow no longer fit a template.
**Service page:** [/services/course-platform-for-creators](/services/course-platform-for-creators)

Custom work makes sense when a proven offer has recurring exceptions: cohorts need different access, partners buy seats in bulk, returning students start elsewhere, or bundles need rules that a hosted tool cannot express.

## What we build

- Checkout and access rules for courses, cohorts, memberships, bundles, team seats, applications, and payment plans.
- A member area, progress view, lesson experience, and support moments that match the programme.
- Practical launch-week admin tools for payments, access, emails, and failed checkout.
- Selected links between sales, access, engagement, and support data.

## Delivery approach

We start with one real offer, design the buyer-to-learner handoff, prove the purchase-and-enrolment loop in weekly builds, then hand over code and documentation.

## Scope and cost

A focused creator platform usually costs **$25,000–$75,000**. Checkout logic, memberships, team purchasing, catalogue migration, and a built-in community add complexity.

## FAQs

### How soon can I sell?
Usually **8–12 weeks**. The purchase-and-delivery path is proven before every optional feature is polished.

### Do I keep my payment processor and revenue?
Yes. The platform connects to the provider you choose and does not take a platform cut above processor fees.

### Can existing courses and students migrate?
Available courses, media, and customer records are exported; buying, access, and refunds are tested with realistic data before switching.
