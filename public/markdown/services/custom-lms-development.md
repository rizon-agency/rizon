# Custom LMS Development

**For:** founders and operations leads whose LMS now shapes the product they sell.
**Service page:** [/services/custom-lms-development](/services/custom-lms-development)

Rizon builds owned learning platforms around the learner journey, commercial model, and systems a team already uses. This becomes worthwhile when enrolments are reconciled in a spreadsheet, checkout exceptions need support intervention, or a vendor's learner flow has become a business constraint.

## What we build

- Learner and admin experiences designed around the programme rather than generic tabs.
- Course publishing, enrolments, roles, reporting, support controls, and correction paths.
- Identity, payments, CRM, video, and LTI connections planned before development.
- Assessment and reporting that answer operational questions, not a wall of metrics.

## How a first release works

1. Map the real learner and operational work in week one.
2. Prove the risky login, enrolment, payment, migration, or reporting path in weeks two and three.
3. Review working software in weekly builds.
4. Test real roles, imports, emails, and recovery paths before handover.

## Scope and cost

A focused first release usually costs **$25,000–$80,000**. Several roles, a large migration, complex reporting, payments, or LTI/SSO integrations can exceed that range. Typical scope includes discovery, UX, learner and admin applications, required integrations, testing, deployment, and handover documentation.

## FAQs

### How long does the first release take?
Most focused platforms are usable in **8–12 weeks**, with working reviews each week.

### Do we own the code?
Yes. Handover includes the agreed application code, deployment documentation, and infrastructure access. Support is available, never required.

### Can it work with existing systems?
Where the API or standard supports it, yes. Identity, payments, content packages, and reporting integrations are confirmed during discovery.
