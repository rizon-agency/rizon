# School and Student Portal Development

**For:** school and university IT leaders who need students, parents, teachers, and staff to use one understandable front door.
**Service page:** [/services/school-student-portal-development](/services/school-student-portal-development)

A school portal should remove the seams between systems, not decorate them. Rizon builds around actual student, guardian, teacher, and administrative workflows while leaving specialist systems in place where replacement is unnecessary.

## What we build

- Role-specific views for students, parents, teachers, and staff.
- Learning, assignment, feedback, gradebook, progress, and reporting flows where scoped.
- SSO, SIS, and LTI planning with an explicit source of truth for identity and enrolment.
- Permissions, audit requirements, retention, and incident responsibilities for student data.

## Delivery approach

We trace a real school day, decide which system owns each record, pilot with real roles, and document governance, support, change approval, and recovery before launch.

## Scope and cost

A focused portal first release usually costs **$35,000–$100,000**. SIS integration, roles, parent access, gradebook requirements, legacy-data migration, and LMS replacement determine the range.

## FAQs

### Do we need to replace our SIS and LMS?
No. The SIS can remain the enrolment source of truth and an LMS can continue to own course content while the portal gives users one front door.

### Who controls student data?
The institution controls the environment and data-access model; permissions, audit requirements, retention, and incident responsibilities are documented before launch.

### How long does launch take?
A focused first release is typically **8–12 weeks**, starting with high-volume paths such as sign-in, timetables, and assignments.
