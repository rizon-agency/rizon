# Corporate Training Platform Development

**For:** L&D and HR leaders who need training linked to roles, SSO, compliance evidence, and employee systems.
**Service page:** [/services/corporate-training-platform](/services/corporate-training-platform)

Rizon builds training platforms for organisations where training is an operational system, not merely a video library. The usual problem is that the organisation chart, identity system, and audit requirements do not match the LMS model.

## What we build

- Role-based access for employees, managers, contractors, partners, and administrators.
- SSO and HR-system integration planning.
- Training assignment, attempts, acknowledgements, expiry, certificates, and evidence.
- Manager reporting that limits access to the right team and answers a real decision.
- SCORM, xAPI, LTI, and specialist-content behaviour defined with acceptance tests.

## Delivery approach

We trace one requirement from policy to proof, connect identity early, test with a real department, and document ownership at launch. A polished dashboard with the wrong access model is expensive wallpaper.

## Scope and cost

A focused first release usually costs **$30,000–$90,000**. SSO, HRIS connections, compliance evidence, partner training, and historic-record migration increase scope. Reporting questions are scoped before estimating because that is where vague requirements become expensive.

## FAQs

### How long until launch?
About **8–12 weeks** for the core, built in weekly slices. Identity and role data are proven first.

### Can it connect to SSO and HR systems?
Usually, yes. The identity provider, systems of record, and sign-in flow are confirmed in discovery.

### How are compliance records handled?
The platform can record attempts, acknowledgements, expiry, certificates, and material versions. Historic records either migrate or move to a retained audit archive before cutover.
