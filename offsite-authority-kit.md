Rizon is a custom learning-platform studio. We help course businesses, schools, and training teams turn an e-learning or edtech idea into a production-ready platform they own.

## LinkedIn post starters

### The workaround test

The moment to consider a custom learning platform is rarely when someone asks for a prettier dashboard.

It is when a workaround touches revenue, learner access, or a weekly operational task. A sales rep promises an offer the checkout cannot express. An admin spends Friday afternoon reconciling enrolments. A manager needs a completion record and has to export three CSV files.

Count that work. It is part of the cost of the current platform, even if it never appears on the subscription invoice.

### The migration warning

An LMS migration is not a content-export exercise.

Courses and files are the visible part. The hard work hides in identity, enrolment history, grades, LTI tools, payments, and the exceptions one person has been fixing quietly for years.

Before promising a cutover date, migrate one difficult course. It tells you more than a polished project plan.

### The ownership point

Owning the code is not a reason to rebuild a working LMS by itself.

It matters when the learning experience, the commercial model, or the operating workflow is important enough that a shared product roadmap keeps making the decision for you.

Sometimes the sensible choice is to keep the LMS. Sometimes the sensible choice is to stop making your team work around it.
