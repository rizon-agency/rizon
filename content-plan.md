# Rizon content plan

## House style

Every article opens with a one- or two-sentence answer, then earns the detail. Choaib Mouhrach writes as the engineer responsible for the build, to one named buyer with a live decision to make. Each post needs a real scenario, a cited primary-source fact where a factual claim needs support, one extractable table or list, a link up to its service pillar, and at least two links sideways to sibling posts.

For AI-search visibility, use question-led H2s where the reader would genuinely ask one, state the direct answer before the explanation, and avoid unsupported performance claims. Source current product, standard, security, and pricing facts from the primary organisation rather than from an affiliate roundup.
